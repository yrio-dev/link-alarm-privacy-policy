Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy defines the handling of personal information in the smartphone application "Link Alarm" (hereinafter referred to as "the App") provided by the developer.

Article 1 (Collection of Personal Information)

The App does not collect or transmit personal information that identifies the user (name, address, phone number, email address, etc.).

Article 2 (Handling of Imported Data)

The App uses schedule data (calendar files, etc.) imported by the user only within the device and does not transmit it to any external server.

Article 3 (Use of Device Features)

The App uses the following device features to provide alarm functionality. These features are used only on the device, and no data is transmitted externally.

  • Scheduling exact alarms
  • Restoring alarms after device restart
  • Displaying notifications
  • Showing full-screen alerts and vibration when alarms ring

Article 4 (Inquiries)

For inquiries regarding the Privacy Policy of the App, please contact us via the email address listed on the Google Play Store page.
